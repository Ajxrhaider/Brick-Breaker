About the game

Brick Breaker is a classic arcade game where you control a paddle to bounce a ball and break bricks.

That's all. This isint a new concept. This is not the first brick breaker you have ever played.

Use the arrow keys to move the paddle left and right. Press the spacebar to launch the ball.

Once all the bricks are gone you can go th the next level or replay the same level.

Play the game and enjoy the obviously original soundtracks(Absolutely NOT original but who cares).


About the developers
